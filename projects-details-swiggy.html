Business Problem & Objective

Logistics operations experienced high operational latency in agent turnaround times (TAT) during peak demand hours. The core objective was to aggregate distributed daily delivery worksheets into a clean central operational viewport to track dispatch bottlenecks and identify underperforming hub sectors.

Metrics & Features Configured

  • Fulfillment Rate Tracking: Dynamic conditional metrics tracking hourly order drop-offs.
  • Turnaround Time (TAT) Matrix: Interactive nested index-match frameworks isolating dispatch delays down to individual regional hubs.
  • Interactive Filter Panels: Built explicitly using standalone spreadsheet slicers and cross-referenced multi-tier data validation menus without relying on heavy scripts.
